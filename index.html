Ilkka Paananen

Ilkka Paananen is the CEO and co-founder of Supercell, the Finnish games company behind hit games such as Clash of Clans, Clash Royale and Brawl Stars. Founded in 2010, Supercell's games have been featuring in the global top 10 for years and are played by more than 250 million people every month.

P.E.K.K.A

The P.E.K.K.A is likely a reference to D.A.V.E. from Backyard Monsters. Both have names that do not stand for anything in particular, both are robotic in nature and both were considered the strongest regular troop in their respective games. According to Pocket Gamer, Clash of Clans was inspired by Backyard Monsters.
